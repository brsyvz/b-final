Since 2011, Vitalen Automotive Inc. has been addressing the needs of our country and the world by keeping up with the changes in technology. Vitalen Automotive Inc., acting in line with 100% domestic and national production, represents Turkey all over the world with its products named Pedal Commander, Chip Werke and Fukin Tuned.

Pedal Commander, which started to be produced in Turkey in 2011, has started to be used all over the world, especially in America and Europe. It received the design award four times in a row at the international SEMA fair. Pedal Commander reached out to more than hundred thousand users in a short time, it is one of the most used Electronic Throttle Control (ETC) devices in the world.

Vitalen Automotive Inc. continues to market its products to the world that strengthen vehicles.

Vitalen Construction, under the umbrella of Vitalen Holding, aims to provide high added value to its investors by building environmentally friendly, advanced technology, world-class buildings, it aims to become the most respected brand in its sector.

It continues to work with the aim of sustainable transformation with its expert staff, the highest quality suppliers and the most reputable solution partners.

Vitalen Agriculture, which acts with the motto of "For a Health-Filled Tomorrows", is a company under the umbrella of Vitalen Holding. It was established to increase the production volume of our country, which is among the best in the world in terms of soil fertility.

Vitalen Agriculture, operates a 100-decares of walnut field in Çorum, İskilip, and a 50-decares of banana greenhouse in Bozyazı, Mersin. Aiming to produce the highest quality, Vitalen Agriculture provides spraying and irrigation systems in walnut land with drainage pools. The latest technology products are used in banana greenhouses.

Vitalen Holding, follows technological developments closely. N-PATH Games is the company under the umbrella of Vitalen Holding, which is established in the field of game technology, where mobile game projects are developed.

Npath Games, continues to produce and market products with a team of 45 experts in their fields.
